The Beautiful Series

A series of pieces dealing with beauty and interaction. In order to learn the fundamentals of physical computing, I created a series of different experiments on the conjunction of interaction and expectations.

He is beautiful, but shy.

The flower only lights up when you cover it with your hand.

You are beautiful, but you're too close.

When you wear the glasses, mirrors drop in front of your eyes.
