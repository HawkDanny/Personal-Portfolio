    NARWHAL PICNIC

    2018

    Game

    VR

    Unity

    Narwhals

    The coolest thing about VR is that the user is blind to the world around them, so I made Narwhal Picnic as an exploration of that design space, with an emphasis on player expression within a scene. It was super sucessful at both creating a very playful experience and also leaving red rings on my friends' foreheads.

    INSTAGRAM.COM/LIT.ROCKS

    2018

    Art

    Shaders

    Unity

    Instagram

    These rocks started as a project to learn to make things look good in Unity, with the stipulation that I use one of the rock models from Low Poly Rocks Pack by LMHPoly. The project was then revived in the summer of 2018 as a way to learn Unity's Shader Graph.

    BEAUTIFUL SERIES

    2018

    Art

    Physical Computing

    Arduino

    Beauty

    A series of pieces dealing with beauty and interaction. In order to learn the fundamentals of physical computing, I created a series of different experiments on the conjunction of interaction and expectations. They all serve to subvert good/expected design.

    MY DOG'S EYES

    2017

    Art

    Creative Coding

    HTML5 Canvas

    Zammuto

    The artist Zammuto wrote a song titled "My Dog's Eyes" and the first time I heard it I stood still. It is my favorite song. The words are steeped in imagery, and I used the words themselves to construct the moments they describe. This piece is my lover letter to the song.
