My Dog's Eyes

“My Dog’s Eyes is based on an outdated list of children’s favorite things. Zammuto took each image, found its rhythmic heartbeat and intrinsic melody, and put them in order of increasing tempo as a structural basis for the track. He then composed additional instrumentation around this structure, in the process revealing the natural cadence and harmony within each phrase" - Temporary Residence Ltd

The words of My Dog’s Eyes are steeped in imagery, and I wanted to crate a marriage between the imagery and the lyrics. So I constructed the images using the literal words. Please go to the full project here.
