GOOD SEX

Good Sex is a video game about communicating your way through a good session of sex. Appropriating game controllers, Good Sex tasks players with standing back to back, where each player's movements on their analog stick vibrate their partner's controller. Work together to simultaneously satisfy each other's controllers in order to level up and beat the game before the timer runs out. Good Sex is a game that treats sex both seriously and playfully, with players quickly finding themselves often checking in with their partners or unintelligibly reacting to their partners' success.
